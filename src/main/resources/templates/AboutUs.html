About Us

WombatFM is a free, wiki-like platform where music fans can collect and share setlists from concerts around the world. It's a space for the community to contribute and explore the songs that bands and artists actually performed live. Whether you're reminiscing about past shows, discovering rare setlists, or gearing up for an upcoming concert, WombatFM has you covered!

Anyone who loves sharing their knowledge about setlists is more than welcome to contribute by adding or editing them. Whether it's correcting a detail, filling in missing songs, or uploading an entire setlist, your input helps keep the community informed and connected. Join us in building the ultimate resource for live music enthusiasts everywhere!
